Tahai River

5.0 10 ratings from Rp5,000 - Rp10,000 Destination

Walking along Lake Tahai creates its own impression. Don't forget to capture the moment with the background of the beautiful lake located in Tahai, Tumbang Tahai, Bukit Batu District, Palangka Raya City, Central Kalimantan.

Kahayan Bridge

4.5 8 ratings from Rp2,000 Destination

The bridge crosses the Kahayan river that flows beneath it. The bridge's unique orange color makes it iconic. Photos of the bridge can be taken in the park located to the southeast of the bridge. The bridge connects the city of Palangkaraya with the northern region of Central Kalimantan.

Taman Nasional Tanjung Puting

5.0 15 ratings from Rp20,000-Rp30,000 Destination

Tanjung Puting National Park is a conservation area in West Kotawaringin that was originally a wildlife reserve and nature reserve.

RM Cianjur

5.0 10 ratings from Rp30,000-Rp55,000 Food

Jelawat fish is used by residents to be processed into appetizing seafood. Before grilling, the fish is seasoned with garlic, lemongrass and ginger, which are also used to make the sauce. The white and soft flesh of the jelawat fish combines with the fresh brown-black sauce, giving a unique taste sensation in just one bite.
